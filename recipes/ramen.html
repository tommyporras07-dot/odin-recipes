Ramen

This dish appears to be a cheesy ramen bowl, featuring instant ramen noodles served in a rich, fiery broth and topped with melted cheese, a soft-boiled egg, and sliced green onions. The combination of spicy broth and creamy melted cheese creates a comforting balance of heat and richness, while the soft-boiled egg adds extra protein and a velvety texture.

This recipe is a quick and satisfying meal that's perfect for busy weeknights or whenever you're craving something warm and flavorful. It can be easily customized with additional toppings such as mushrooms, spinach, kimchi, corn, or cooked chicken. The result is a hearty bowl of ramen that's both comforting and packed with bold, savory flavors.

Ingredients

For the Ramen

  • 1 package instant ramen noodles (with seasoning packet)
  • 2 cups water or chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (Korean chili paste) or 1 teaspoon chili paste (optional, for extra spice)
  • ½ teaspoon garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

For the Toppings

  • ½–1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 soft-boiled egg, halved
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes or chili oil (optional)
  • Cooked mushrooms, spinach, corn, kimchi, or sliced cooked chicken (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the broth:

    2. In a medium saucepan, bring the water or chicken broth to a boil. Stir in the ramen seasoning packet, garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, and gochujang (if using).

  2. Cook the noodles:

    3. In a medium saucepan, bring the water or chicken broth to a boil. Stir in the ramen seasoning packet, garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, and gochujang (if using).

  3. Add the cheese:

    4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the hot noodles. Cover the pot for 1–2 minutes to allow the cheese to melt into the ramen.

  4. Prepare the egg:

    5. While the noodles are cooking, boil an egg for 6–7 minutes for a soft-boiled center. Peel and cut it in half.

  5. Assemble the bowl:

    6. Transfer the cheesy ramen and broth to a serving bowl. Top with the soft-boiled egg and sliced green onions. Add sesame seeds, chili flakes or chili oil, and any optional toppings such as mushrooms, spinach, corn, kimchi, or cooked chicken.

  6. Serve:

    7. Enjoy immediately while the ramen is hot, with the cheese still melted and the broth rich and flavorful.
